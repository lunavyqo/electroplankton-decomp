# Credits

Symbol names, struct layouts, and toolchain knowledge may build on community
reverse-engineering. This file tracks that provenance.

## Rule

**Import knowledge, write code.** You may use known symbol names and field
offsets. All C promoted to `src/` must be hand-written from scratch against your
own legally dumped ROM. Do not paste source from other decomp projects.

## Tools and projects we learn from

- [ds-decomp (dsd)](https://github.com/AetiasHax/ds-decomp) — DS decomp toolkit patterns
- [sm64ds-decomp](https://github.com/tangosdev/sm64ds-decomp) — matching workflow and legal scope model
- pret and other NDS decomps — mwccarm idioms and community RE practice
